Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

VisionMessenger is built on a simple principle: your private conversations should stay private. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the minimum information necessary to operate the service:

  • Phone number — used to create and verify your account, and to allow other users to find you if they have your number in their contacts.
  • Device information — device model and OS version, used for troubleshooting and push notification delivery.
  • Usage metadata — approximate timestamps of when messages are sent and delivered (not message content).

We do not collect or store the content of your messages, voice notes, or calls. These are end-to-end encrypted and only accessible to you and your intended recipients.

2. How We Use SMS

SMS text messages are used exclusively for:

  • Account verification when you sign up or log in
  • Security alerts and two-factor authentication codes

We will never send you marketing messages, promotions, or unsolicited SMS communications.

3. End-to-End Encryption

All messages, media, and voice notes exchanged through VisionMessenger are protected by end-to-end encryption. This means:

  • Messages are encrypted on your device before transmission
  • Only the intended recipient can decrypt and read them
  • VisionMessenger servers never have access to your message content
  • Encrypted calls are established directly between participants

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share limited data only in these circumstances:

  • Legal compliance — if required by a valid court order or applicable law. Because message content is end-to-end encrypted, we cannot provide it even if compelled to do so.
  • Service providers — infrastructure partners (cloud hosting, push notification services) who process data strictly on our behalf and under confidentiality agreements.

5. Data Retention

Message content is not stored on our servers after delivery. Account metadata (phone number, device tokens) is retained for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting support.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Withdraw consent for optional data processing

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@visionmessenger.app.

7. Security

We use industry-standard security practices including TLS encryption for all data in transit, encrypted storage for account data at rest, and regular security reviews. However, no system is perfectly secure — we encourage you to use a strong device passcode and keep your app updated.

8. Children's Privacy

VisionMessenger is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of VisionMessenger after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us at support@visionmessenger.app.
